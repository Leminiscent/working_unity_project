--------------------
Package Information

Battlers, SV Battlers and Sprites folders (Original & 2X/3X) contains 8 sprites split by the following categories:
- Amazon Warrior
- Dark Elf Male
- Dark Elf Female
- Dragonian Girl
- Fox Miko
- Orc Female
- Tiefling Female
- Warforged

Portrait folders (Original & 2X/3X) contains:
- Busts subfolder with 8 large portraits for each character.
- 8 facesets, with 8 portraits each.

Extras folders contains:
- SV Weapons folder, at original source side for overlay options.
- SV Battler weapons for RPGMV/MZ Engines.
